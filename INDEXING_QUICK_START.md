## Your Website is Ready for Indexing! ✅

---

## STEP 1: Google Search Console (REQUIRED)

**Time needed**: 10 minutes

1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Sign in with Google
3. Click "Add Property"
4. Select "URL prefix"
5. Enter: `https://trustedescort.in`
6. Verify ownership (HTML tag method is easiest)
7. Submit sitemap: `https://trustedescort.in/sitemap.xml`
8. Done! ✓

**What happens next:**
- Google will crawl your pages within 24-48 hours
- All 962+ pages should be indexed within 2-4 weeks

---

## STEP 2: Bing Webmaster Tools (RECOMMENDED)

**Time needed**: 10 minutes

1. Go to: https://www.bing.com/webmaster
2. Sign in with Microsoft account
3. Click "Add a site"
4. Enter: `https://trustedescort.in`
5. Verify ownership
6. Submit sitemap: `https://trustedescort.in/sitemap.xml`
7. Done! ✓

**Benefits:**
- Yahoo and DuckDuckGo use Bing's index
- Additional traffic source
